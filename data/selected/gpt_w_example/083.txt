The immortal spent countless lifetimes longing for the moment he could finally find a way to die and be reunited with Death, his eternal love. He tirelessly pleaded with the universe, hoping for mortality to embrace him once more. Despite trying various methods—venturing into dangerous lands, seeking out wise sages, and consulting ancient beings—life stubbornly refused to let go. His desperation grew stronger with each failed attempt; he crossed treacherous terrains and questioned the universe’s design. Yet, every effort only revealed how deeply cursed his existence truly was. Still, the immortal persisted, guided by his unwavering love that shone like a guiding light through endless ages. Eventually, at the height of his despair, he understood that the true reunion he sought wasn’t in death after all, but in cherishing the eternal bond they shared, finding peace in their timeless connection.
