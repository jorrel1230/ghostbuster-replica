Years went by; she grew into a bright, curious girl whose laughter lit up every room. But life grew harsh and unkind, turning her into a target in a world that branded her a monster simply for standing up for herself. The last time she called you "mama," her voice shook with a mix of despair and profound pain. The world betrayed her, twisting her truth into a lie.
